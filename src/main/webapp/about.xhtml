Project Description

The "Tribal Handicraft" project aims to support tribal communities by selling their exquisite handicraft products, thereby providing them with a sustainable source of income and improving their livelihoods. Through this initiative, the project seeks to showcase the unique craftsmanship of these tribal artisans to a broader market, fostering appreciation for their cultural heritage while empowering them economically. By promoting and selling tribal handicrafts, the project endeavors to create a positive impact on the lives of these marginalized communities, promoting social inclusion and preserving their traditional art forms.
